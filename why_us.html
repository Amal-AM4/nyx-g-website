Not Just PCs – Solutions Built for Your Success.

Welcome to NYX-G STORE – Kerala’s Trusted Custom PC & Tech Solutions Hub. At NYX-G STORE, we specialize in Custom Gaming PCs, Crypto Mining Rigs, Workstations, Servers, and Tech Accessories. Based in Kerala with services across Trivandrum, Palakkad, and beyond, we’ve been helping individuals, gamers, and businesses since 2021.

Our mission is simple – deliver high-performance, reliable, and budget-friendly systems that fit your exact needs. Whether you’re a gamer chasing frames, a business needing server power, or a creator editing on deadlines, we’ve got the right solution for you.

We don’t just sell parts – we provide end-to-end support including setup, rentals, upgrades, and 24/7 technical help. With 1000+ custom systems delivered, we’ve built a reputation for trust, expertise, and customer-first service. At NYX-G, it’s not about pushing hardware – it’s about building your system, your way.

The Man Behind the Mind

NYX-G STORE was founded in 2021 by Askar Hussain, a self-taught tech enthusiast from Kerala with a passion for building PCs that truly make a difference. Askar’s journey didn’t begin in a fancy office or with big investors — it started just like many of our customers: with curiosity, limited resources, and a burning drive to push the boundaries of technology. From helping friends assemble their first gaming rigs to fine-tuning mining setups late at night, he learned every detail hands-on.

But what set him apart was his belief:
Technology should be accessible to everyone, not just the rich or the elite.

Under his leadership, NYX-G STORE has grown from a small local initiative into a trusted name across Kerala and beyond — delivering 1000+ custom PCs, mining rigs, and workstations that match not just performance needs but also the dreams and goals of everyday users.

For Askar, every build isn’t just about hardware. It’s about understanding people — their struggles, their ambitions, their future. That’s why NYX-G STORE doesn’t just sell machines. It builds trust, confidence, and systems that truly belong to the customer.

Askar Hussain
Founder, NYX-G STORE
